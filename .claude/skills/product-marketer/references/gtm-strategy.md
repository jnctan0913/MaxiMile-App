## Product Lifecycle Marketing Deep Dive

### Introduction Stage

**Goals**: Build awareness, educate market, acquire early adopters

**Strategies**:
- **Pricing**: Penetration pricing (low to gain share) OR skimming (high for early adopters)
- **Promotion**: Heavy investment in education, PR, content marketing
- **Distribution**: Selective -- target channels where early adopters live
- **Product**: Focus on core MVP features, rapid iteration based on feedback

**Key Metrics**:
- Awareness (brand recall, media mentions)
- Trial / sign-up rate
- Early adopter satisfaction (NPS)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)

### Growth Stage

**Goals**: Increase market share, establish brand loyalty, optimize unit economics

**Strategies**:
- **Pricing**: Competitive pricing, introduce tiers/plans
- **Promotion**: Shift from education to differentiation. Competitive advertising
- **Distribution**: Expand channels, add partnerships
- **Product**: Feature expansion based on user feedback, improve quality

**Key Metrics**:
- Revenue growth rate
- Market share
- Customer retention / churn rate
- Lifetime value (LTV) / Customer acquisition cost (CAC) ratio

### Maturity Stage

**Goals**: Defend market share, maximize profit, extend lifecycle

**Strategies**:
- **Pricing**: Value-based pricing, loyalty discounts, bundles
- **Promotion**: Brand loyalty campaigns, retention marketing, upsell/cross-sell
- **Distribution**: Maximize coverage, optimize underperforming channels
- **Product**: Variations, extensions, premium tiers, platform plays

**Key Metrics**:
- Profit margins
- Customer retention rate
- Revenue per customer (ARPU)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

### Decline Stage

**Goals**: Manage decline strategically, maximize remaining value

**Strategies**:
- **Harvest**: Reduce costs, maintain profitability with existing customers
- **Divest**: Sell off product or business unit
- **Pivot**: Reinvent or repurpose the product for new market/need
- **Niche down**: Focus on the most profitable remaining segment

**Key Metrics**:
- Revenue decline rate
- Profit per customer
- Cost to serve
- Migration rate (to new product, if applicable)

## GTM Motions

### Product-Led Growth (PLG)
- **Definition**: Users discover, adopt, and expand usage on their own (self-serve)
- **When to use**: Horizontal SaaS, developer tools, consumer apps
- **Key tactics**: Free trial/freemium, in-product onboarding, viral loops
- **Metrics**: Activation rate, PQL (Product Qualified Lead), expansion revenue

### Sales-Led Growth (SLG)
- **Definition**: Sales team drives customer acquisition and expansion
- **When to use**: Enterprise, high ACV, complex products, regulated industries
- **Key tactics**: Demo-driven sales, account-based marketing, POC/pilot
- **Metrics**: Pipeline generation, win rate, deal size, sales cycle length

### Community-Led Growth (CLG)
- **Definition**: Community drives awareness, education, and advocacy
- **When to use**: Open-source, developer tools, passion-driven categories
- **Key tactics**: Community building, user groups, contributor programs
- **Metrics**: Community size, engagement rate, community-sourced leads

### Marketing-Led Growth (MLG)
- **Definition**: Marketing campaigns drive demand and qualified leads
- **When to use**: Mid-market SaaS, established categories
- **Key tactics**: Content marketing, demand gen, lead scoring, nurture
- **Metrics**: MQLs, pipeline contribution, CAC, attribution

## AARRR (Pirate Metrics) for GTM

Map your GTM activities to the AARRR funnel:

| Stage | Metric | GTM Activity | Goal |
|-------|--------|-------------|------|
| **Acquisition** | How do users find us? | SEO, paid ads, PR, partnerships | Drive traffic/downloads |
| **Activation** | Do users have a good first experience? | Onboarding, in-product guidance, welcome emails | Convert visitors to users |
| **Retention** | Do users come back? | Email campaigns, push notifications, feature releases | Keep users engaged |
| **Revenue** | How do we make money? | Pricing, upselling, cross-selling | Generate revenue |
| **Referral** | Do users tell others? | Referral programs, social sharing, NPS follow-up | Organic growth loop |
